It's time to rethink patient recall management

Effective patient recall makes absolute clinical and business sense. Whether your goal is to improve clinical care or boast practice revenue, effective patient recall management is at the heart of it all.


Create recalls easily


creating recalls is not the same as simply creating an appointment. Following a visit, an On Schedule recall is created with associated information such as the summary of the patient visit, and the reason for the patient recall. This is part of what makes recall more than just an appointment booking process.

After sending a recall reminder on the recall date, A Reminder recall is created for a subsequent future date. This reminder recall can be updated untill the patients next visit where a new on schedule recall will be created.

With our patient recall manager, its goodbye to manually trying to sort through your recall list on a daily basis.

On creation, a recall is automatically added to your recall list and sorted according to the date of recall.

This way, you always find the recalls for the day on top of the list. this saves you the time and energy spent manually trying to sort through your list.

Manaully sorting through your recalls is primarily responsible for the ineffectiveness in many recall systems.

Patient recall/management history at your fingertips


Our patient recall manager allows you to search and view the recall history for every patient.

This allows you have information about the patient management timeline. By clicking on the patients ID, you can access the recall detail page, where more information such as the summary of the patient's visit, date of previous visit, reason for recall and the patient phone number. This functionality allows our recall manager suffice as a simplified EHR system.
